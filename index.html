Enlightenment

April 2026

As per our current worldview, we believe that we are living in modern times, we have modern medicine, internet, AI at our disposal. Easy transportation and longetivity.

But, was this belief held by all the previous generations as well? as in imagine, in ancient indian civilisation,some 2000 years ago, were those people also claiming that they are leaving in modern times, compare to the past, as they have advanced medicine ( ayurveda ), transportation through carts and carriages, and a good understanding of human health!

Did soldiers, in WW2 felt, after seeing the advanced warfare that they were living in the most modern times, humanity has witnessed? As I am thinking this about WW2, Is there anyone from future thinking about 2026? If yes, then -> yes, the claim is held in 2026, we believe that we are living in the best, and most modern years, humanity has ever faced!
